Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 24, 2026

What's In My Fridge AI ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

TL;DR: We only collect what's needed to run the app. We never sell your data. You can delete everything at any time.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you sign in with Google, we collect:

  • Email address (via Google Sign-In)
  • Display name (via Google Sign-In)
  • Google account ID (for authentication only)

Food & Kitchen Data

The data you enter into the app is stored to provide our services:

  • Food item names, categories, and expiry dates
  • Grocery list items
  • Saved recipes and preferences

This data is linked to your account and stored securely in Firebase Firestore.

Photos & Images

When you use the AI scan feature:

  • Photos are sent to Google Gemini AI for food identification
  • Photos are not stored on our servers or in your account
  • Photos are processed in real-time and discarded after analysis

App Settings

  • Notification preferences (stored locally on your device)
  • Theme preference (light/dark/system)
  • Sort and filter preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Authentication: To create and manage your account
  • Core Features: To track food items, expiry dates, and grocery lists
  • AI Features: To identify food items from photos and suggest recipes
  • Notifications: To send expiry alerts you've enabled

3. Third-Party Services

Google Firebase

  • Authentication: Google Sign-In for secure account management
  • Firestore: Cloud database for storing your food items and preferences
  • Data is encrypted in transit and at rest per Google's privacy policy

Google Gemini AI

  • Used for food identification from photos and recipe suggestions
  • Photos and text are processed via Google's API and are subject to Google AI terms
  • We do not store or log AI requests beyond what Google retains per their policy

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, sell, or share:

  • Precise location data
  • Contact list or social media data
  • Browsing history
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Any data for advertising or marketing purposes

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Your data is stored in Firebase Firestore with encryption at rest and in transit
  • Only you can access your food items (secured by Firebase Security Rules)
  • App preferences are stored locally on your device using Android DataStore
  • We follow industry-standard security practices

6. Your Rights & Data Control

You have full control over your data:

  • Export: Export all your data anytime from Settings → Export Data
  • Delete: Clear all your data anytime from Settings → Clear All Items
  • Account deletion: Delete your account by contacting us (see below)
  • Sign out: Sign out anytime from Settings → Sign Out

7. Children's Privacy

What's In My Fridge AI is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has used our service, please contact us and we will promptly delete the data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you by updating the "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
