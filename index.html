Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 24, 2026

What's In My Fridge AI ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

TL;DR: We only collect what's needed to run the app. We never sell your data. You can delete everything at any time.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you sign in with Google, we collect:

  • Email address (via Google Sign-In)
  • Display name (via Google Sign-In)
  • Google account ID (for authentication only)

Food & Kitchen Data

The data you enter into the app is stored to provide our services:

  • Food item names, categories, and expiry dates
  • Grocery list items
  • Saved recipes and preferences

This data is linked to your account and stored securely in Firebase Firestore.

Photos & Images

When you use the AI scan feature:

  • Photos are sent to Google Gemini AI for food identification
  • Photos are not stored on our servers or in your account
  • Photos are processed in real-time and discarded after analysis

App Settings

  • Notification preferences (stored locally on your device)
  • Theme preference (light/dark/system)
  • Sort and filter preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Authentication: To create and manage your account
  • Core Features: To track food items, expiry dates, and grocery lists
  • AI Features: To identify food items from photos and suggest recipes
  • Notifications: To send expiry alerts you've enabled

3. Third-Party Services

Google Firebase

  • Authentication: Google Sign-In for secure account management
  • Firestore: Cloud database for storing your food items and preferences
  • Data is encrypted in transit and at rest per Google's privacy policy

Google Gemini AI

  • Used for food identification from photos and recipe suggestions
  • Photos and text are processed via Google's API and are subject to Google AI terms
  • We do not store or log AI requests beyond what Google retains per their policy

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, sell, or share:

  • Precise location data
  • Contact list or social media data
  • Browsing history
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Any data for advertising or marketing purposes

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Your data is stored in Firebase Firestore with encryption at rest and in transit
  • Only you can access your food items (secured by Firebase Security Rules)
  • App preferences are stored locally on your device using Android DataStore
  • We follow industry-standard security practices

6. Data Retention

  • While your account is active: We retain your account information and food/kitchen data for as long as your account exists so we can provide our services.
  • After account deletion: We retain your user data for up to 30 days after account deletion, after which it is permanently and irreversibly deleted from our systems and Firebase databases.
  • Photos: Photos you submit for AI food identification are ephemeral — they are processed in real-time and are not stored on our servers or in any persistent storage. No photo data is retained after the analysis is complete.
  • Local device data: App settings and preferences stored locally on your device are not affected by account deletion. You can clear them by uninstalling the app.

7. AI Training — Your Data Is Not Used for Model Training

Important: Your photos and food data are NOT used to train, improve, or fine-tune Google Gemini AI models or any other machine learning models.

  • Photos sent to Google Gemini AI are used solely for the purpose of identifying food items in your fridge at your request.
  • Your food items, grocery lists, recipes, and preferences are not shared with Google or any third party for AI training purposes.
  • Google Gemini processes your requests under API terms that prohibit using customer data for model training. For details, see Google AI terms.

8. Your Rights & Data Control

You have full control over your data:

  • Export: Export all your data anytime from Settings → Export Data
  • Delete: Clear all your data anytime from Settings → Clear All Items
  • Account deletion: Delete your account and all associated data at any time via the self-serve option in Settings → Delete Account within the app. Upon deletion, your data is retained for up to 30 days (see Section 6) and then permanently erased.
  • Sign out: Sign out anytime from Settings → Sign Out

9. GDPR — Rights for European Union Users

If you are a resident of the European Union or the European Economic Area, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of Access: You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you and to obtain a copy of that data.
  • Right of Rectification: You have the right to request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete personal data we hold about you.
  • Right of Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data. You can exercise this right at any time by using Settings → Delete Account in the app, or by contacting us at marulabsupport@gmail.com.
  • Right to Data Portability: You have the right to receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format. You can export your data at any time via Settings → Export Data.
  • Right of Objection: You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data on grounds relating to your particular situation. Where applicable, we will cease processing unless we have compelling legitimate grounds.
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: If you believe that our processing of your personal data infringes GDPR, you have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority in the EU Member State of your habitual residence, place of work, or place of the alleged infringement.

Legal Basis for Processing: We process your data based on (a) your consent when you create an account and use our services, (b) the necessity of processing for the performance of the contract (providing the app's features), and (c) our legitimate interests in operating and improving our service — though this never includes using your data for AI model training.

10. CCPA — Rights for California Residents

If you are a resident of California, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to Know: You have the right to request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you in the past 12 months.
  • Right to Delete: You have the right to request that we delete any personal information we have collected from you, subject to certain exceptions. You can exercise this right via Settings → Delete Account in the app or by contacting us at marulabsupport@gmail.com.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale: We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information for monetary or other valuable consideration. Therefore, there is no need to opt out of a sale.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA rights.

Categories of Information Collected (past 12 months):

  • Identifiers: Email address, display name, Google account ID (collected when you sign in)
  • Commercial information: Food items, grocery lists, and recipes you enter
  • Internet or network activity: Usage data processed by Firebase for app functionality

To submit a verifiable consumer request under CCPA, please contact us at marulabsupport@gmail.com. We will respond within 45 days as required by law.

11. Children's Privacy

What's In My Fridge AI is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has used our service, please contact us and we will promptly delete the data.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you by updating the "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, or want to exercise any of your rights (including data access, deletion, or portability requests), please contact us at:

  • Email: marulabsupport@gmail.com

For GDPR-related inquiries, you may also lodge a complaint with your local EU supervisory authority. For CCPA-related inquiries, submit a verifiable consumer request to the email above and we will respond within 45 days.
